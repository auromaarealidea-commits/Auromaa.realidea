Our Mission
SAFETY IS AN ATTITUDE

"Inculcating the attitude of Safety for all through Training on Fire and Safety."

Founded in 2003, Auromaa Fire Fighting Equipment Mfg & Services began with a clear purpose — to champion fire safety awareness across communities and industries alike.

With twenty-five years of combined experience alongside pioneers in the firefighting field, our team brings unmatched expertise to every project — from individual fire extinguisher maintenance to complete firefighting system installations.

Our core commitment: fulfilling customer expectations with consistent effort, hard work, and delivering work ON TIME, EVERY TIME.
